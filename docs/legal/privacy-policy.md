# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-07-07

---

## Who We Are

Claude Compass is a free, open-source plugin for Anthropic's Claude Cowork, maintained by Myo Win Thein. It provides two slash commands that help globally-minded job seekers research countries for remote employment or visa sponsorship and estimate realistic local-market salaries. The source code is available at [github.com/myowinthein/claude-compass](https://github.com/myowinthein/claude-compass).

---

## What Data We Collect

Claude Compass does not collect data on behalf of the developer. All data the plugin handles stays in your own Cowork workspace on your device.

During a session, the plugin writes the following files locally:

- **`profile.md`** — structured information extracted from the resume you provide
- **`situational-profile.md`** — your current location, citizenship, languages spoken, and any known immigration friction
- **`.country-finder-state.json`** / **`.salary-calculator-state.json`** — pipeline progress, so sessions can resume after interruption
- **`remote-candidates.md`**, **`sponsorship-candidates.md`**, **`country-research.md`** — salary and visa research you paste in during a session

No data from these files is transmitted to the developer or to any server operated by the developer.

---

## How We Use Your Data

The developer does not receive, process, or store any personal data from plugin users. The files listed above are created and read solely by Claude (Anthropic's AI) within your Cowork workspace to support the plugin's pipeline steps.

Where processing occurs within Anthropic's infrastructure (your messages, pasted resume, and research data passing through Claude), the legal basis under GDPR Article 6(1)(b) is **performance of a contract** — processing is necessary to provide the plugin's functionality you requested.

---

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

The developer shares no personal data with any third party.

When you use Claude Compass, your inputs are processed by **Anthropic** through their Claude Cowork service. Anthropic operates under its own privacy policy: [anthropic.com/privacy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).

The Claude Compass documentation site (hosted on GitHub Pages) loads resources from:

- **jsDelivr** (`cdn.jsdelivr.net`) — serves the Mermaid diagram library. jsDelivr may log your IP address as standard CDN infrastructure.
- **myowin.dev** — serves the site favicon. This domain may log your IP address.

These third parties operate under their own privacy policies. The developer has no access to logs or data held by these services.

---

## International Data Transfers

The developer transfers no data internationally, as no data is collected.

When you use the plugin, your data is processed by Anthropic, whose infrastructure may be located outside the EEA. Anthropic's privacy policy describes the safeguards that apply to those transfers.

---

## Data Retention

All files Claude Compass creates (`profile.md`, state files, research files) live in your Cowork workspace and are retained until you delete them. You can delete these files at any time from within your workspace. The developer retains no copies.

---

## Your Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under GDPR:

- **Access (Art. 15)** — request confirmation of whether personal data concerning you is processed and a copy of that data
- **Rectification (Art. 16)** — request correction of inaccurate personal data
- **Erasure (Art. 17)** — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction (Art. 18)** — request that processing be restricted in certain circumstances
- **Portability (Art. 20)** — receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Objection (Art. 21)** — object to processing based on legitimate interests

Because the developer holds no personal data, these rights apply primarily to data held by Anthropic (governed by their policy) and the CDN providers listed above. To exercise rights against Anthropic, contact them directly at [privacy.anthropic.com](https://privacy.anthropic.com).

---

## Children's Privacy

Claude Compass is not directed at children under the age of 16. If you are under 16, please do not use this plugin. The developer does not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## Supervisory Authority

Under GDPR Article 77, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in the EU member state where you live, work, or where an alleged infringement occurred. A list of EU data protection authorities is available at [edpb.europa.eu](https://www.edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/about-edpb/members_en).
