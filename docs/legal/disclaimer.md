## No Professional Advice

Nothing produced by Claude Compass constitutes legal advice, immigration advice, or financial advice.

- **`/country-finder`** identifies countries worth researching for remote hire or visa sponsorship based on criteria and research you supply. It does not advise you on your legal eligibility for any visa, your rights under immigration law, or whether any specific employer will sponsor you.
- **`/salary-calculator`** estimates local-market salary ranges based on research you provide. It does not advise you on employment contracts, compensation negotiation strategy, tax obligations, or financial planning.

If you are making significant decisions about relocation, visa applications, salary negotiation, or employment offers, consult a qualified immigration lawyer, financial adviser, or employment specialist in the relevant jurisdiction.

---

## AI-Generated Content

Claude Compass runs on Anthropic's Claude. The country scores, salary calculations, confidence assessments, and all other output it produces are generated by a large language model.

AI models can make errors, misread evidence, draw incorrect inferences, or produce output that sounds confident but is factually wrong. This is true even when Claude is working from real research data you have supplied. You should treat all output as a starting point for your own judgement, not as a definitive conclusion.

Do not act on Claude Compass output without independently verifying the claims that matter most to your decision.

## Accuracy and Completeness

Claude Compass is designed to work from research you supply rather than from Claude's training data. However:

- The accuracy of its output depends entirely on the accuracy and recency of the research you paste in.
- Visa policies, salary benchmarks, immigration rules, and hiring practices change frequently. Research that was accurate when gathered may no longer reflect current conditions.
- Claude Compass does not verify, fact-check, or cross-reference the research you provide. It processes what you give it.

No guarantee is made that any output is complete, current, or correct.
