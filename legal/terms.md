## What Claude Compass is

Claude Compass is a plugin that provides two structured slash commands: `/country-finder` and `/salary-calculator`. It helps you research countries for remote employment or visa sponsorship, and calculate realistic local-market salary expectations, using research you supply and ground yourself.

Claude Compass does not provide legal, immigration, financial, or employment advice. See the [Disclaimer](disclaimer.md) for details.
